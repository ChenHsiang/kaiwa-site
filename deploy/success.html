What to do next?

Configure your DNS

You will need to add an A record to your DNS with the IP of the server.

Connect to Otalk

Once your DNS configured, you can connect to Otalk by browsing to yourdomain.com using your username (without the domain) and password.

Add users

Connect to Otalk using the 'admin' account and the administrator password you've provided to add user accounts in the LDAP directory.

Configure other clients

Your XMPP server address will be your domain name and your username username@yourdomain.com.
